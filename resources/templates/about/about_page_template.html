Client-side Encryption

All your Passwords and Secure-Notes are encrypted and decrypted in the Browser/Client

Why should you care?
Client-side encryption means that your data never exists in its plain (unencrypted) form outside of your computer.

Using Passman on a sketchy network?
No problem. Your plain data does not travel over the network.

Passman's servers get compromised?
No problem. Even our own engineers (or anybody else with admin access) cannot read your data (even if they wanted to)

Bottom line: Your data is only readable by you
